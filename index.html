MyoBionics

MyoBionics is a project to interface Thalmic Labs Myo™ ArmBand with the Open Bionics Ada hand.

Hand prosthesis varies widely in shape, size, function and price. However we pose a solution that intends to make prosthetics more flexible. So forget about all the struggles of getting these two things to work and let us guide you through the process.
