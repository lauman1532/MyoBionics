What is MyoBionics

The name MyoBionics arises from merging Thalmic Labs Myo Armband and Open Bionics. Hence, as the name suggests, MyoBionics is a project to implement the Myo Armband myoelectrical control system to operate an Open Bionics 3D printed hand by a team of second year bioengineers at Imperial College London.

The main objective of MyoBionics is to provide a system interfacing of a MyoBand and Ada hand in primary. However, this may also extend to other forms of user interface systems not limited only to prosthesis but other daily usage items.
